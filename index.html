Introducing HIIGH ON

Let's be honest, being a founder in today's world can feel like shouting into a void. You've poured your heart and soul into building something incredible, yet when it's time to grow faster you need to find investors, you’re often met with a wall of noise. The rules of the game seem to be about referrals and connections, not about the actual merit of your product.

“Sometimes life is going to hit you in the head with a brick. Don't lose faith.”

— Steve Jobs

I know this frustration firsthand. As a founder, I experienced a world where traction and innovation took a back seat to who you knew. It's a system that can leave many brilliant ideas unseen, simply because the person behind them doesn't have the "right" connections. That’s why I decided to build a different kind of network.

Introducing HIIGH ON

HIIGH ON isn't like any other social media platform. It's a founders social network built exclusively for founders. This isn't a place for random posts or personal updates. It's a focused, professional space where the only thing that matters is what you’re building. Whether you're a seasoned founder with a revolutionary product or a first-time founder with a small but promising project, this space is for you.

Our mission is simple: to connect founders with not just a investors but with a great mentor in a way that’s meaningful and fair. We've cut through the noise so that you can find the funding you need without getting lost in the crowd.
